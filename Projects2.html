Farmer's Market Finder

My team and I designed a sustainable focused technology, Farmer's Market Finder, on Figma for a class project. The website is targeted towards helping the target audience, people who grocery shop,locate fresh produce at local markets as an approachly to reducing the impact of climate change through smart shopping.


Timeline:
4 Months

Tools:
Draw.io
LucidChart
Role:
UI/UX Designer
Researcher

Problem


"How can we encourage individuals who buy their own groceries to shop more at locally sourced markets to minimize the impact of their shopping habits on the environment?"



Goals


"The goal is to adopt more environmentally sustainable practices that will have a positive impact on the world."




Solution



1. Create an Account — users can create an account from 2 options, 'Customer' or 'Business Owner'

2. Search Markets — users can type in specific keywords to find markets in the search bar.

3. Locate Nearby Markets — users are prompted to share location or enter in zipcode, search markets with side navigation bar, and when the cursor is hovered over a market, their business profile link will appear.

4. Find Profile Page — customers can view market information -- location, address, operation hours, current open status, review, etc.

5. Write Google Reviews — users can read previous reviews and write new reviews with a brief description and rating on a scale of 1 - 5.

6. Submit a Question — users can voice by submitting their questions through the 'Help' page.

Persona
— a character that represents the target audience for a product or service

Meet Sarah, a busy working professional who prioritizes convenience over everything else. She does her grocery shopping wherever is most convenient for her, without giving much thought to where the products come from. However, she is starting to become more aware of the impact of how she shops and is looking for ways to make a change. Sarah is the perfect target for the "Farmer's Market Finder" website as she seeks to balance her busy lifestyle with her desire to live a more sustainable life.



Our team conducted interviews with 12 individuals belonging to various groups - college students, adults, parents, and elders - to gain insights into our target audience. The findings revealed that the typical user is a busy professional or student who values healthy living, hobbies, spends time with friends & family. Eating healthy is considered important, but only when it is both convenient and affordable for them.



Define


Problem Scenarios
— a problem description through stories of an user's experience

Meet Sarah, a busy working professional who values the convenience of shopping at the grocery store nearest to her office. She often relies on pre-packaged meals and ready-to-eat snacks, as they are quick and easy to prepare. However, Sarah is becoming increasingly concerned about the negative impact her shopping habits have on the environment and her health. She wants to find a way to continue eating healthily while also reducing her carbon footprint. Sarah is struggling to find affordable and convenient sources of fresh produce, and is looking for a solution that will help her make more informed and sustainable shopping choices.

In the designing process, I considered the challenges Sarah faced in finding affordable, healthy, and readily available food options and her desire to reduce her carbon footprint. Our team outlined several ideas to address these problems, including the encouragement of shopping at the local farmer's market. Customers can purchase high-quality food that are sourced locally, thereby reducing the negative environmental impact of transportation. By supporting local farmers, users like Sarah can access fresh, whole foods that are better for their health and the environment.

Reflections:

After I completed the Farmer's Market Finder website, I understood the general process of what it’s like to be a Human Centered-Design & Development major. Student Tangible & Relaxing
Solutions

My team and I designed an AI-based technology with a focus on improving mental health and productivity among college students to present at the Nittany AI Case Competition. The mobile application analyzes users’ data to personalize mental health exercises (i.e. take a breath) and effective study sessions based on their schedule.


Timeline:
1 Month


Tools:
Figma
Role:
UI/UX Designer

Problem



"How can technology be used to enhance mental well-being?"



Goals



Our objective is to improve mental health by focusing on increasing user productivity, motivate users to practice mental health activities through social engagement, and improve overall well-being.




Solution



1. Personalized Calendar — displays a monthly calendar and suggests personalized study sessions with breaks based on user's personal calendar and consistent use of scheduling tool.

2. Social Media — users can share 'accomplishments' with friends when a daily health suggesiton is completed and engage when others express their success as well.

3. Daily Health Suggestion — suggests personalized health activities based on individuals' data, using heart rate, screen time, schedule, interests, etc.

Reflection



Working on STARS increased my awareness of the difficulty that surrounded mental health. There is no one solution that can be the answer that everyone is looking for, but there are many things I believe that can lead a person to a better life. With this project, I reflected on habits that made a positive impact on my productivity and mind. From my experiences, I understood that changing one single habit can make a significant change in my life and that can be as simple as taking a deep breath or drinking more water. I took consideration into my team members' & I's personal experience, alongside research, that led to the solutions we designed for STARS. I learned that collaborating with a group can be challenging because there needs to be clear communication, organization, and leadership in order for us to execute our goal efficiently. I also learned that it can be rewarding because of what we can create with everyone working on the same side for the same goal.
