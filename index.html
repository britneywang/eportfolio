About Me



Hi, my name is Britney Wang. I am majoring in Human Centered Design & Development and minoring in Diversity & Inclusion in Health with the goal of creating inclusive solutions for all individuals. My portfolio showcases my past projects I worked on for my studies and competitions.

Alongside my academic career, I am passionate about photography, travel & food. I started my food & travel journey through social media where I share my experiences & discoveries that are impactful to my life with my audience.

Over the summer, I interned in Sydney, Australia where I learned the value of intentional living, how to align my passions and goals in life, and the ability to create & add value to anything with limited resources. I discovered the butterfly effect and realized how a small action can make an atomic impact. The enlightening and motivation experience drives me to make a positive impact in this world.

Currently, I am seeking an internship or co-op position where I can continue pursing my passion in creating technological solutions to help others.
